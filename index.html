Introduction

My desired research direction is policy evaluation: The impact of marijuana policy on criminal rate in U.S.

Background of marijuana legalizationï¼š

The scope of marijuana legalization can be divided into two aspects: recreational legalization and medical legalization.

Legalized marijuana using means that the cost of acquisition of marijuana is relatively low and crimes associated with illegal transaction and acquisitions would reduce. As a result, the criminal rate tends to reduce due to marijuana legalization.

With a growing number of states considering the legalization of medical marijuana and, more broadly, the decriminalization of recreational marijuana, this Forum event examined the public health pros and cons. What does science tell us about the benefits and risks of using marijuana? What are the economics at play? And what impact might decriminalization make on social justice?
